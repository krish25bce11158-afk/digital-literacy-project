# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

✔ Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts  
✔ Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) whenever possible  
✔ Never share OTP, PIN, or bank details with anyone  
✔ Always check website URL before entering login information  
✔ Avoid clicking suspicious email or SMS links  
✔ Do not download files from unknown websites  
✔ Use secure and trusted payment apps only  
✔ Regularly update software and antivirus  
✔ Be careful when using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions  
✔ Report cybercrime on https://cybercrime.gov.in  
✔ Call helpline number 1930 for financial fraud complaints  

These safety measures help reduce the risk of online fraud and protect personal information.
