# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for Students

## Do's
✔ Use a professional and appropriate profile picture  
✔ Think carefully before posting any content online  
✔ Respect other people's opinions and communicate politely  
✔ Keep your personal information private  
✔ Use strong passwords for social media accounts  

## Don'ts
✘ Do not share your OTP, passwords, or sensitive information  
✘ Do not post offensive, abusive, or harmful content  
✘ Do not click on suspicious links or unknown messages  
✘ Do not spread false or unverified information  
✘ Do not upload inappropriate photos or videos publicly  

Following these guidelines helps maintain a positive digital reputation and ensures safe use of social media platforms.
